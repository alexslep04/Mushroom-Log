    Help & FAQ

    Everything you need to know about using Mushroom Log.

    How do I start a new journal entry?
    When you open the app a blank draft is ready for you. Fill in the journal name, date, location, and observation notes. If you want to start fresh at any time, click the Clear Entry button.
    How do I add photos?
    Click the Add Photo button on the right side of the log form. You can select one or multiple images from your device. A preview will appear and the app will automatically send them to the mushroom identification API.
    How does mushroom identification work?
    Once you upload a photo, the app sends it to the Kindwise mushroom.id API through a local server proxy. The top 3 matches are shown with confidence percentages. You can click Use this result to attach a match to your log entry.
    How do I save a log entry?
    Click the Save Log button at the bottom of the form. You must have at least some notes or a photo before saving. Saved entries are stored in your browser's localStorage and can be viewed on the Collections page.
    How do I view my saved entries?
    Click the Collections button in the top right corner. All your saved logs will be listed there. Click on any entry image to load it back into the log form.
    What does the Undo button do?
    The Undo button reverses your last action, such as adding or removing a photo or editing your notes. It only tracks actions within the current session.
    How do I export a log?
    Click the Export Log button to download a plain text file with your journal name, date, location, notes, photo filenames, and top mushroom matches.
    How do I use my current location?
    Check the Use current location checkbox. Your browser will ask for permission to access your location. If granted, your coordinates will be saved with the log entry.
